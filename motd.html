Terms of Service

By Playing on the Infinity Network, you agree to certain rules and terms, that you must follow in order to be allowed to play.

By playing on the Infinity Network, you agree to follow ALL rules stated by this ToS and agree that if you are caught breaking a rule, you will be banned.

- Reporting Rulebreakers -

If You see someone, that you feel is breaking the Terms of Service, please contact an admin by saying @ in chat. For an example, '@admin awflewaffle is RDM'ing!' This will help us fix the issue faster. If no admins are on, please note the players name, and the time, and send it to an admin as soon as one is online. (In-game, or Discord.)

- Basic Rules -

  1. Do not prop kill other players (Throwing or dropping props on or at players)
  2. Do no use hacks.
  3. Do not use glitches to enter someone's base.
  4. Do not set your spawn inside or around someone else's base.
  5. Do not spawnkill.
  6. Do not block off someone's base.
  7. Do not build in someone else's base.
  8. Do not argue with staff members.
  9. Do not prop push.
  10. Do not spam.
  11. Do not build obscene or offensive items in publicly viewable areas.
  12. Do not use offensive language.
  13. Be nice to all players/staff
